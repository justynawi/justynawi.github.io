I am a Business Intelligence and Analytics professional with experience spanning reporting, finance, audit, and data-driven decision support in international organizations. Over the course of my career, I have developed and delivered reporting solutions that improve visibility, reduce manual effort, and support better business decisions. My core focus is on Power BI, SQL, DAX, and data modeling, with particular interest in building scalable dashboards and translating complex business requirements into clear analytical solutions.

My background combines strong analytical foundations with practical business understanding. Experience in audit and finance shaped my approach to data quality, controls, and financial logic, while later roles in business analysis and team leadership strengthened my ability to work with stakeholders, define reporting needs, and drive reporting transformation. I am particularly interested in roles where business context, analytics, and effective communication need to come together in a meaningful way.
